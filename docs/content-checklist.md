# Content Checklist

This checklist must be executed before publishing any case study or technical essay.

## Pre-Publication Checklist
- [ ] Read through to ensure calm, explanatory tone (no exclamation marks, no bragging).
- [ ] Verify that the Executive Summary is between 100 and 150 words.
- [ ] Confirm Key Takeaways contains exactly 3 bullets.
- [ ] Verify that no paragraph exceeds 90 words.
- [ ] Confirm all headings contain a maximum of 5 words.
- [ ] Check that at least one architecture flowchart diagram is present.
- [ ] Confirm there is at least one trade-off table showing Decisions, Benefits, and Costs.
- [ ] Verify there is at least one reflection on what went wrong (What Didn't Work).
- [ ] Check for at least one active unanswered question at the end.
- [ ] Ensure all referenced metrics are authentic and measured.
