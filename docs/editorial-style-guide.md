# Editorial Style Guide

This style guide documents the tone, formatting, and structural constraints for all written content on nikhilbajantri.dev.

## Tone & Voice
- **Calm and Explanatory**: Use calm engineering language. Avoid exclamation marks or aggressive rhetoric.
- **Never Claim Expertise**: Do not use self-aggrandizing words such as `expert`, `guru`, `ninja`, or `world-class`.
- **Explain Before Concluding**: Always present constraints and research findings before stating the chosen design decision.
- **Prefer Examples Over Definitions**: Explain abstract technical concepts using concrete code references, network logs, or configuration properties.

## Typography & Formatting Constraints
- **Headings**: Maximum 5 words per heading.
- **Paragraphs**: Maximum 90 words per paragraph.
- **Lists**: Maximum 5 items per list to preserve reading rhythm and limit density.
